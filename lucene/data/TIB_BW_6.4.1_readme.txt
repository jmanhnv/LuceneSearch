Introduction 

TIBCO ActiveMatrix BusinessWorks(TM) is TIBCO's flagship
Enterprise integration offering. Deployed by thousands of
customers worldwide, it serves as an integration foundation for
mission critical IT environments.  

Powered by a next-generation foundation that includes an
Eclipse-based design-time, powerful process engine and a
modular OSGI-based run-time, BusinessWorks 6 enables developers
to create new services, orchestrate business processes and
integrate applications in the shortest time possible.
